We need boots on the ground there first. An infantry squad—Designation: Team Anvil—is currently staging at the Northern End of your tarmac. You are ordered to taxi immediately, load them into your cabin, and scramble to the northwest.

Ground crews won't have time to re-vamp your whole rack, but they are sprinting out to strap short-range air-to-air missiles to your pylons right now. You are going to have to fight for the airspace.

Tune in to 515 khz FPR, get Anvil loaded up and get up there now!
