# Silkworm Site

Operation Sea Spray

## SW Intro

Vanguard, this is Overlord. Turn-around is complete. Ground crew is pulling clear of your skids now. You are cleared for immediate departure on Operation Sea Spray.

Your target is a hostile Silkworm anti-ship missile battery threatening our naval fleet. It's dug in right on the coast, just south of Kemi. If those missiles launch, our boys in the water are sitting ducks.

Set your ADF receiver to NDB Frequency 575 kHz SWT. Fly directly to that beacon. Once you are over the SWT station, come to a hard heading of 180 degrees and push outbound for 8.5 klicks.

You have authorization to switch all targets to scrap metal. Neutralize the launch rails and radar guidance vehicles before they can lock onto our ships. Move fast, pilot. Good hunting. Out.

## SW Complete

Vanguard Overlord

Outstanding work. Direct hits confirmed on satellite imagery. The fleet has a clean lane now. But do not celebrate yet, your tasking isn't done. You need to head back to base immediately.

Vanguard, switch your ADF receiver to 950 kHz. That frequency will give you a direct homing beacon straight back to FARP London. Execute a direct approach route over the water to avoid any remaining coastal stragglers.

Land, hot refuel, and load right back up with a heavy air-to-ground payload. Bad news just came down from HQ: the Air Force is experiencing massive logistical delays and cannot support the next phase. The entire inland push is falling squarely on our shoulders.
