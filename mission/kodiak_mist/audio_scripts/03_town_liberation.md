# Town Liberation

Operation Quiet Citadel

## TL Intro

### Prompt

Firm and highly cautionary. The emphasis on "precision strikes only" and "collateral damage" should sound like a strict warning, not just a casual reminder.

### Script

Vanguard, this is Overlord. Ground crews are clear. You are cleared for immediate takeoff on Operation Quiet Citadel. The clock is ticking, pilots.

Your destination is the town of Simo. Intel confirms Russian forces are unboxing mobile SAM systems right now. If those air defenses go active, they will shut down this entire theater.

Set your ADF receiver to NDB Frequency 550 kHz. Track that beacon directly to the target area. Keep your eyes open as you approach the city limits.

It's tight, Vanguard. The enemy is dug in right among the civilian blocks. Remind your gunner of the Rules of Engagement: precision strikes only. We cannot afford collateral damage. If you level that town, the locals will turn against us.

Objectives:

- Tune ADF to 550 khz
- Destroy the enemy forces
- Be careful of collateral damage

## TL Enemy Reinforcements Inbound

### Prompt

Urgency. The "Break, break!" should completely cut off or immediately follow the pilot's calm report. They should speak rapidly but clearly

### Script

Break, break! Vanguard, Overlord. Abort your victory lap! We have a critical flash override from tactical satellite intelligence! Emergency alert!

Sat-intel has just picked up a massive Russian mechanized column sprinting toward your position. They are executing a coordinated, multi-axis assault to retake the town. Armor is ingressing right now from the Northeast, the East, and the Southeast!

They are moving at maximum combat speed, Vanguard. You have five minutes until enemy armor crosses the town boundaries.

Repeat: five mikes until impact. You are the only barrier between that armor and the civilian population.

Enemy Reinforcements ETA: 5 minutes

## TL Civilian Revolt

### Prompt

Gravely serious and direct. This is a political and tactical disaster, so the voice should sound heavy with defeat but firm on the evacuation order.

### Script

Vanguard, Overlord. Abort the mission immediately! Stand down! Tactical intelligence confirms the civilian population in Simo has revolted. They've had enough of the destruction of their town. The locals have taken to the streets with machine guns and RPGs.

Command has just declared a strict No-Fly Zone across the entire Simo sector. All allied aircraft are ordered to clear the airspace until specialized SEAD missions can be deployed to suppress the threat.

Sat-intel estimates you have exactly five minutes to evacuate the area before the newly captured SAM launchers are fully set up and operational. If you are still in that airspace in five mikes, you will be shot down.

RTB immediately.

## TL Complete

### Prompt

Warm, genuinely appreciative, and supportive. Delivering a success message after an intense battle should make Command sound proud of the flight crew's discipline

### Script

Phenomenal work, Vanguard! Sat-intel confirms the remaining hostile infantry is retreating northward in total disarray. You just saved that town and kept our inland campaign alive. Outstanding precision flying, pilots!

Change of plans. You are being rerouted to Kemi-Tornio Airfield for your return to base. The logistics pipeline has opened up there, and they have the facilities to support our next operational phase.

Tune your ADF receiver to Frequency 415 kHz. Confirm you are pulling the identifier KTA. Follow that beacon directly to the airfield tarmac.

You've earned a breather, Vanguard. Land the birds, shut down the powerplants, and get your crew inside for a quick meal. You’re going to need the energy. While you’re eating, a fresh intelligence briefing will be coming down the pipe for Stage 4.
