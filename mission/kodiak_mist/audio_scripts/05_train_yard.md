# Train Yard

Operation Steel Junction

## TY Intro

Overlord to Vanguard

Russian logistics units are unboxing heavy anti-aircraft technicals across the Tornio Railyard. They are clearing the tracks for a massive armored supply train heading south from the border.

The railyard's automated switching beacon is active. Tune your auxiliary ADF receiver to Frequency 775 kHz. Confirm you are pulling the identifier TYT.

Remember: smash the garrison and the technicals, but do not destroy the primary tracks or electronic switches. We need that rail line intact for our own logistics. Clear the yard before that train arrives. Overlord out.

Vanguard, Kemi Tower

You are cleared for immediate tactical departure to the west. The clock is running against us on this one, pilot. No time to waste on ingress. Switch to primary tactical command once you clear the airfield perimeter. Out.

TLDR:

- Tune ADF to 775 khz TYT
- Ingress to the city of Tornio
- Eliminate the enemy forces occupying the rail yard
- DO NOT DESTROY INFRASTRUCTURE

## TY Civilian Uprising

Vanguard Overlord

Abort your attack run! Stand down immediately! Tactical intelligence confirms total mission failure. You exceeded the collateral damage threshold. The rail switches are ruined—we cannot use this infrastructure!

The enemy supply train has halted outside the town and is deploying mobile air defense assets directly onto the line! Our foothold in Tornio is completely lost. We are ceding the sector!

Sat-intel estimates enemy SAMs will be fully active in five minutes. If you are still in that airspace when those radars spin up, you are as good as dead. Evacuate immediately and return to Kemi-Tornio Airfield!

## TY Complete

Outstanding tactical flying, Vanguard! Sat-intel confirms the enemy supply train has halted three klicks north of the border—they’ve realized they have no station to offload into and are retreating! You just broke the back of their logistics pipeline.

Vanguard, you are cleared to break station and return to base. Excellent work out there today. Set your auxiliary ADF receiver to Frequency 415 kHz and lock onto the KTA beacon. Track it straight back to Kemi-Tornio Airfield.

## Kemi Tower
